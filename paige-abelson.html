About Us > Team > Paige Abelson

Paige Abelson

Estimator

Paige Abelson has been with Select Sand & Gravel for three years, where she provides estimates for residential and commercial customers. Her focus is on asking the right questions, learning the details of each project, and ensuring customers get the right materials at the right price, on time.

Before joining Select, Paige worked for another logistics company, where Select was one of her clients. When that company closed, she was invited to join the team—and has been helping customers ever since. Skilled in both estimating and dispatch, she combines technical knowledge with customer service to make every job run smoothly.

One project Paige is especially proud of was the Engage Five development at Texas Motor Speedway. Born in Galveston and a Fort Worth resident for 15 years, she enjoys reading, working out, and doing crafts. Paige and her husband have three children and two pit bulls, Marley and Biscuit.

Advice to Customers:

My best advice is to take measurements of the area you're working on. With those details, I can accurately estimate how much rock or sand you'll need to get the job done right.
