About Select Sand & Gravel

Your One Call for Material, Delivery & Peace of Mind

At Select Sand & Gravel, we don’t just sell material—we solve problems. Whether you're a contractor trying to keep your crew moving or a homeowner managing your first project, we make it easier to get the right material, from the right place, delivered at the right time.
You don’t need to make 10 calls to different pits, haggle over pricing, or guess if your order will show up on time. We handle it all.

We Simplify the Complicated

Sourcing sand, gravel, topsoil, or fill dirt from multiple pits across Texas can be a logistical headache. That’s where we come in.

We Increase Availability

We’re not tied to one pit—we work with dozens. That means if one location is out or overloaded, we have multiple backup options to keep your job moving without delay.

We Improve Pricing & Quality

Our network allows us to compare pricing, material type, and delivery timelines in real time—so you get the best material at the best value, not just whatever’s nearby.

We Handle the Scheduling

From coordinating loads across counties to dealing with weather delays, we handle all the dispatch complexity so you don’t have to. One call to us, and your delivery is in motion.

We're Built Around Your Job Site

We understand construction schedules, last-minute changes, and the pressure of keeping jobs on track. Our dispatchers and logistics pros are trained to anticipate problems and keep you informed.
