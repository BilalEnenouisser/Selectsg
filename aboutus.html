About Us

Select Sand & Gravel

Founded in the early 1980s, Select Sand & Gravel has become a leading sand and gravel supplier in Texas and Oklahoma. In June of 2022, Select Sand & Gravel proudly became an employee-owned company and is a Teamshares network company. In addition to sand and gravel, the products we deliver include Fill Dirt, Topsoil, Road Base, and Crushed Rock to Dallas, Fort Worth, Austin, San Antonio, Houston, Midland/Odessa, and Oklahoma City.

The company has always been operated with the intent to provide customers with the confidence that we WILL deliver the requested construction or project materials when needed and as promised. We strive to accommodate our customer's requests which include:

  • • Sand, gravel, rock, topsoil hauling for customer projects;
  • • Custom orders for non-standard aggregate, rock, sand, or soil materials. Examples include: Colorado River Rock, Decomposed Granite, Mexican Beach Pebble, Decorative Landscape Rock, and Cement Treated Sand.
  • • Construction Site Hauling available using End-Dump Trailers, Dump Truck & Pup, and Tandem Dump Trucks
  • • Haul-off of construction debris and dirt spoils
